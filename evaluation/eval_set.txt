Guru

Gaesatae

13) What are the benefits that Alan Turing provides?
Great Scientist who Triggers the Eureka for Computers and 1 random technology from the Modern era. If Computers technology is already triggered, instead completes Computers technology.

14) What does Matterhorn do?
Land combat units who move next to the Matterhorn ignore Hills for the rest of the game.

15) What is the Migration Treaty?
+20% faster Population growth but -5 Loyalty per turn in this player's cities.
or +5 Loyalty per turn but -20% Population growth in this player's cities.
